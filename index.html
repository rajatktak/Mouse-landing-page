VicVisionX

Voice writing

By pressing the convert key as you speak, the chip in VicVisionX understand you perfectly, it will process your speech into an on-screen text format and automatically type it out into your computer with 95% accuracy. For a speed recorder, perhaps it can type 60 words in one minute, but VicVisionX is double or triple. It is really a great assistant for clerks, teachers, students, writers or recorders…

Voice translation

English,Mandarin Chinese and Cantonese can be translated into 24 language text. After choosing the language on Mouseper software, by only pressing the convert key when you speak, your voice will be translated and typed out into the language text format as per your selection. The things you want to say are easily displayed on your computer with a foreign language.

Voice command

Very often, after we installed much software on our computer we might come across difficulty to find the exactly software we want. Mouseper provide a good solution for this problem. By setting the command word and relevant website/software/folder on our Mouseper software, you can control the program by pressing the search key while you speak the command word.

Voice search

This is a very convenient feature of Mouseper for you to search anything you want on the Internet. You just need to press the search key and speak what you want to search, weather, news, video, shopping or literally anything. Your default browser will execute your speech command to do the job.
